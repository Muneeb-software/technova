Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services, products, and engagement process.

What industries do you serve?

We work with fintech, healthcare, e-commerce, manufacturing, government, and startups any organization that needs scalable, secure technology.

Do you offer fixed price projects?

Yes for well defined scopes. For complex AI/cloud projects, we usually start with a discovery phase and move to time & materials or milestone billing.

What is your typical project timeline?

Small MVPs: 4–8 weeks. Enterprise platforms: 4–12 months depending on scope. We provide detailed timelines after discovery.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes optional maintenance & support plans include SLA based response times, monitoring, updates, and scaling assistance.

How do we get started?

Fill out the contact form or email hello@technova.com we’ll schedule a 30 minute discovery call within 24 hours.
